Our Story

Clean and Supply Southwest was established in 2023 by Ian Clifford. For several joyful months, Ian and his daughter, Kaylie, operated the business together, building a foundation based on trust, quality, and family values.   In early 2024, our family faced a heart-wrenching challenge when Ian received a terminal cancer diagnosis and passed away a few months later. Despite the immense loss, Ian's wish was for his daughter, Kaylie, to lead the business and uphold his legacy.   Today, Kaylie dedicates herself to honouring her father's memory with unwavering hard work and dedication. She strives to ensure that Clean and Supply Southwest continues to embody the principles and values Ian cherished, serving our community with the same passion and commitment.
